About

Introduction

My name is Davud Dautbasic and I am a student at Florida State University and I am currently obtaining a Bachelors degree in Information Technology. My expected graduation date is Summer 2019.

Upcomming

I was born in the small Eastern European country of Bosnia and Herzegovina in 1997, and moved to the United States November of 2000. For a short duration, I lived in New York, before moving to Florida. Here, I spent most of my life enjoying the weather, and working on my education with the persistance of my parents.

Education

After graduating from Ridgewood High School, I decided to attend Pasco Hernando State College for my general education requirments. Towards my second year at PHSC, I worked towards obtaining all of my prerequistes towards getting admitted to the IT program at Florida State University. In 2017, I made the leap to a state university and I could not be happier.

With graduation around the corner, I am looking for internships or full time positions in the fields of User Experiance, Database Managment, Web Development, and IT Security.
